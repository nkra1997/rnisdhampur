RN INTERNATIONAL

The institution, Rn International School, who believed that an institution can never be run by a hand but it requires numerous hand and of course, head and hearts too and it has proven completely true in this case. They were greatly inspired by the saying, “Nothing is impossible.” They wanted the school to be an instrument for including values of universal brotherhood and cultivation of virtues. The school’s motto “Enter to learn and leave to serve” is in fact its guiding mission. It is to inspire the children and the young youth to think in terms of welfare of all people to whom they come in contact with directly or indirectly.

RN INTERNATIONAL TRANSPORTATION

Transportation and Parking Services offers the Rn International School community a single location for all transportation and parking information. Students, faculty, staff and visitors will find information on Rn International School parking rules and regulations, inter-campus transportation, commuting aids, maps, directions, and a host of links to the greater Rn International School area. Transportation and Parking Services coordinates and/or assists in the coordination of all transportation-related services and programs for special events, athletic events, handicapped access and parking, signage, way-finding, all rideshare activities, and many other related services.
